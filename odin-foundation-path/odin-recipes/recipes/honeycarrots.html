Honey Roasted Carrots

Description

Whether you need a tasty side dish for your holiday dinner or simply for a weeknight meal, Honey Roasted Carrots are an amazing choice.

The glazed carrots are sweet enough that your picky eaters won't even realize they're veggies â€” and you'll feel great knowing they're getting some nutrients. Plus, they're oh-so-easy to make! It doesn't get much better than that.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Honey
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices (Salt and Pepper)

Directions

  1. Choose small- to medium-sized carrots that are all about the same size. Wash and peel the carrots, then place them in a baking dish.
  2. Evenly coat the carrots with olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bake until the carrots are tender, about 30 minutes. If you like softer carrots, add a few extra minutes to the bake time.
